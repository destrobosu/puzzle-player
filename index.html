How it works
White pieces are pre-set and locked in place.
Black cells are obstacles — blocked slots.
Drag player pieces into empty slots to build a path.
Only connected pieces count toward stars.
Disconnected pieces score nothing.
Connected pieces earn MEM points by rarity: Common 10, Rare 20, Epic 30, Legendary 40.
IN → OUT path = 1 star. All filled = 2 stars. All connected = 3 stars.
